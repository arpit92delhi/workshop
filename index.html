About the Tour

This exclusive 2-day workshop is designed for photographers of all levels who wish to capture the soul of Varanasi. You'll work directly with Arpit Narain Saxena, a renowned visual artist and photographer specializing in travel and documentary photography.

From golden sunrise sessions at the Ganges Ghats to intimate street photography in narrow alleyways, every moment is a learning opportunity. We focus on composition, light, cultural sensitivity, and storytelling through images.
